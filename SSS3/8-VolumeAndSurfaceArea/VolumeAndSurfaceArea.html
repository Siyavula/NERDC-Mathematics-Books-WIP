The surface area of an object is the outside surface of the shape which you can cover or paint (like covering a table with a cloth or painting the outside of a building). In the case of a sphere, the surface area is like the surface of the Earth in the picture below. The surface is covered by water and land, which you can see easily in this photo.

(Image from http://eclipse.gsfc.nasa.gov/)

The formula for the surface area of a sphere is A=4πr2A=4πr2. You need to memorise this formula!
